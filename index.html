Pro Tips

  • Use very cold Diet Coke to preserve fizz.
  • Start with 1 tsp–1 Tbsp sugar-free coconut syrup; adjust to taste.
  • Stir gently after adding soda to avoid flattening.
  • For adult versions, add dark spiced rum (1–1.5 oz) after citrus, before soda.
